About DAASH

Redefining Talent
Through Technology

We are a technology-first recruitment and staffing company on a mission to make hiring faster, smarter, and more human — powered by proprietary SaaS platforms built from the ground up.

Our Story

Built by HR Professionals,
Powered by Engineers

DAASH was founded on a simple insight: the recruitment industry has incredible people but outdated tools. While talent professionals work tirelessly to connect great candidates with great companies, the technology that powers them has lagged behind.

We set out to change that. By combining deep HR domain expertise with a dedicated in-house engineering team, we built a recruitment platform that doesn't just digitize old workflows — it reimagines them. Our proprietary SaaS technology automates sourcing, enhances matching accuracy, and provides real-time visibility into every stage of the hiring process.

Today, DAASH serves clients across IT, BFSI, healthcare, manufacturing, and e-commerce — delivering talent outcomes that traditional agencies simply can't match.
