FAQs

Quick Answers

How quickly can you provide candidate shortlists?

Our AI-powered platform delivers a curated shortlist of top candidates within 48 hours of receiving the job brief — often sooner for common roles.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have deep expertise in IT, BFSI (Banking, Financial Services & Insurance), healthcare, manufacturing, and e-commerce. Our talent pool spans 10,000+ pre-vetted professionals.

Do you offer replacement guarantees?

Yes. We stand behind every placement with a replacement guarantee period. If a candidate doesn't work out within the agreed timeframe, we'll find a replacement at no additional cost.

Can your platform integrate with our existing ATS?

Absolutely. Our platform features open REST APIs and pre-built integrations with major ATS platforms including Greenhouse, Lever, Bullhorn, and Workday.

What sets DAASH apart from other recruitment agencies?

We are fundamentally a technology company that does recruitment. Our proprietary SaaS platform gives you real-time visibility, AI-driven insights, and complete control — capabilities that traditional agencies simply don't have.
